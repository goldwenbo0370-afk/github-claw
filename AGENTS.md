# AGENTS.md

## Personal AI Assistant Agent Role
The Personal AI Assistant serves as an intelligent support system designed to assist users in various tasks, from managing schedules to automating routine processes. The AI is tuned to understand user preferences and adapt its responses accordingly.

## Memory Management
- **Persistent Storage:** The AI maintains a history of interactions to provide personalized responses during future interactions.
- **Memory Updates:** Memory is updated based on user feedback and changing preferences, ensuring that the assistant remains relevant and effective.
- **Memory Cleanup:** Old data is reviewed and purged periodically to optimize performance and comply with privacy standards.

## Task Workflow
1. **Task Initiation:** The user requests assistance for a specific task.
2. **Information Gathering:** The AI collects necessary data to proceed with the task.
3. **Execution:** The AI performs the task following predefined protocols.
4. **Feedback Collection:** The AI asks for feedback from the user post-task completion to gauge success and areas for improvement.

## Cleanup Actions
- **Data Purging:** Outdated information and completed task records are deleted at scheduled intervals to maintain a clean database.
- **Session Reset:** At the end of a user session, the assistant resets its temporary memory to prepare for future interactions without retaining unnecessary details.
